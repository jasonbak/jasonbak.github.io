Professional info

I am a legal software developer intern at Fulcrum GT in Chicago, Illinois building an engine to standardize and clean legal data to allow for applications to be more easily built upon it. My team and I are using OWL and RDFS to create an ontology for our MarkLogic GraphDB, SPARQL to create dynamic queries, and ASP.NET MVC 5 Entity Framework to populate MarkLogic with a dynamic model of a law firm.

I am also in my third-year at Carnegie Mellon University studying statistics/machine learning and computer science as an undergraduate student.
